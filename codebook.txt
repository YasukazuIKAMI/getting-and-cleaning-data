This is the explanation of tidy data set with the average of each variable for each activity and each subject.

variable_name	explanation
subject		subject ID. 1~30. factor variable. Subject ID represent each participants.
activity	Descriptive name of activity. There are 6 levels (WALKING ~ LAYING).
--
following 66 variables are the average Acceleromater or Gyrometer variable. 
Averaging was carried out with respect to each subjects and activity.
So there are 180(=30 subjects and 6 activities) averaged observations times 66 variables.
Details of the explanation of each variable are presented in 'features.txt' and 'features_info.txt' in 'UCI HAR Dataset' directory.
